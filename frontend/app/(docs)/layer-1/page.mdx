# Layer 1 — ERC-3643 SPV

Layer 1 holds the legal claim. It is an ERC-3643 (T-REX) permissioned security token representing fractional ownership of the SPV that holds the underlying milestone payment right. Eight contracts deployed and Etherscan-verified on Ethereum Sepolia. **27/27 Foundry tests passing.**

## What lives here

The SPV operating agreement establishes the on-chain token register as the definitive record of membership interests. Four contracts implement that register; four MilestoneToken instances are spawned by the registry constructor (one per backtest program: sotorasib, adagrasib, vepdegestrant, BI-1701963).

## Why ERC-3643 over ERC-20

A fungible ERC-20 token cannot satisfy Reg D 506(c) transfer restrictions on-chain. The compliance logic must live in the token contract itself, not in an off-chain middleware layer. ERC-3643 builds this in via four interlocking modules:

- **Identity Registry** — maps wallet addresses to verified identity claims (accredited investor status, jurisdiction, sanctions screening).
- **Compliance Module** — checks the registry on every transfer; enforces lock-up periods and jurisdiction restrictions.
- **Token Contract** — the asset token, with transfer hooks calling the compliance module before any balance change.
- **Claims Issuer** — the trusted entity (SPV administrator or third-party KYC provider) that signs identity attestations.

This is the same standard used by Securitize for BlackRock's BUIDL fund and KKR's tokenized private equity offerings. The implementation here uses Tokeny's open-source T-REX reference implementation directly — not a custom rewrite.

## Modular permissioning

The permissioning architecture is deliberately modular: venue-level access control (who can participate in the prediction market) is separated from asset-level transfer restrictions (who can hold the SPV token). The two layers may operate under different regulatory regimes and have different liquidity requirements.

- **Venue-level permissioning** is enforced at the Layer 2 prediction market on Base.
- **Asset-level permissioning** is enforced by the ERC-3643 compliance module on Ethereum.

A retail wallet can hold and trade Layer 2 outcome shares without ever being whitelisted on Layer 1. An accredited investor can hold the Layer 1 SPV token without ever interacting with Layer 2. The two memberships are uncoupled.
